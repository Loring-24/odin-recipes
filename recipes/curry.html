Curry Recipe

Description

My personal favorite, Japanese Style curry!

Done right, this dish has a wonderful thick texture somewhere in the vicinity of a stew, with fragrant flavors and plenty of veggies to boot! Served with rice, this dish is a delight.

Recipe as follows:

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Meat
  • Curry Seasoning Blocks
  • Salted Butter
  • Stock (Chicken or Veggie)
    • Chicken
    • Beef
  • Optional:
    • Celery
    • Broccoli
    • Jalapenos (or other pepper)
    • Herbs (Cilantro, Parsley, Bay Leafs, whatever you want really!)

Cooking Steps

  1. Prep your ingredients
    • Heat cooking pot on medium high
    • Chop veggies to bite-size chunks
    • Chop meat similarly
    • Start the rice cooker
  2. Sear the meat in the butter in batches
  3. Once meat is seared, add in the veggies and sear them for a few minutes, no longer than five minutes
  4. Add in stock and meat, stir then set heat to high. Bring to a boil
  5. Once boiling, lid the pot and turn the heat to low. Simmer for about 20 minutes, or until veggies are fork-tender
  6. Give final stir, then serve over rice
  7. Enjoy, I guess
