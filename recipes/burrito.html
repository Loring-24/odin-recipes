Refried Bean Burritos

Description

Nice and simple, beans and tortillas to fill you up.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Refried Beans
  • Cheese
  • Rice
  • Optional for Flavor:
    • Cheese
    • Onions & Garlic
    • Hot sauce, preferably Cholula

Cooking Steps

  1. If using optional ingredients;
    • Shred some cheese
    • Dice onion & garlic; briefly sear
  2. Heat the refried beans
  3. Heat the tortillas
  4. Lay a foundation of bean upon a tortilla; top with optional ingredients
