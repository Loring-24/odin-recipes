Fried Rice

Description

Clean-up crew: leftover rice and whatever you wish to throw in.

Ingredients

  • Leftover rice (or freshly cooked rice)
  • Any veggie, any meat
  • Largest pan you have

Cooking Steps

I'm not kidding, this thing is literally toss whatever you want in a pan.

  1. Dice your ingredients
  2. Set your pan to medium-high
  3. In order from what takes longest to cook to what takes shortest to cook, add in your veggies
  4. Add in rice
  5. Fry and stir until cooked through
  6. Serve
